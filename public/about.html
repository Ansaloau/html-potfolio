About Me

Hello and welcome to my website!

Thanks for stopping by, I'm thrilled to have you here. My name is Ana, and I'm a professional with a diverse background. Originally from Madrid, Spain, I pursued my passion for Journalism and completed my studies there. Throughout my career, I've had the opportunity to work in various domains such as PR, corporate events, meetings and events organisation, and corporate communications, collaborating with different esteemed companies.

Four years ago, I embarked on an exciting journey and made Australia my home. During this time, I've been working for the same company, gaining valuable experiences and skills. Recently, I decided to take a leap and give my professional path a new direction. I pursued a Master's degree in MBA and Corporate Communications, opening doors to new possibilities.

My current focus is on web development, a field that has captivated me with its boundless opportunities for creativity and problem-solving. I'm passionate about learning and have already begun my journey into the world of web development. My goal is to become a proficient web developer, capable of crafting both personal and professional projects with precision and flair.

One thing that sets me apart is my love for design and an unwavering attention to detail. I find joy in bringing ideas to life and ensuring they are not only functional but also aesthetically pleasing. Getting things done in a tidy and beautiful manner is something I take pride in.

Outside of my professional pursuits, I am a proud cat mom. Furry companions have a special place in my heart, and they bring immense joy to my life.

Thank you for visiting my website, and I hope you enjoy exploring my work and projects. Feel free to connect with me; I'm always excited to collaborate and learn from others in this ever-evolving world of web development.
